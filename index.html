The Process

We can buy your business in under 60 days.

We keep things simple and respect your time. Here's exactly how it goes.

Day 1

Intro Call

A 30-minute call to hear your story, what you've built, and what you're hoping for next. No pressure, no commitment.

Day 2

Mutual NDA

We both sign a mutual non-disclosure agreement so every detail of your business stays confidential.

Day 3–5

Simple Info Share

We ask for a short, plain-English list — typically high level financials (revenues, basic costs)

Day 7

Letter of Intent

We send a clear term sheet with a fair valuation and structure. If it feels right, we both sign a Letter of Intent to align before diligence begins.

Day 7–55

Due Diligence

We confirm the handful of things that matter most — we are owners ourselves and share your goal of minimizing any disruption to your daily operations

Day 60

Sign and Close

We sign the purchase agreement and wire you the funds. You hand off a business you love to people who'll care for it.
